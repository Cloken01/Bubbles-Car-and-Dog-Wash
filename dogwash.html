Self-Serve Dog Wash

A clean, enclosed wash room just for pets—no bending over a bathtub at home. Bring your dog, your leash, and a towel; we provide the rest.

Easy-Access Dog Wash Room

Located right next to the bays with a bright red "DOG WASH" sign and glass entry door. Open 24 hours for your convenience.

Raised Tub & Non-Slip Floor

The raised tub keeps you comfortable while washing. Hoses are mounted to the walls, and there’s plenty of room to move around.

Pet-Friendly Settings

Select from tearless shampoo, conditioner, flea & tick treatment, rinse, vacuum, and dryer. All settings are controlled by the simple push-button panel.

How It Works
  1. Secure your dog with a leash and gently guide them into the tub.
  2. Deposit coins or bills into the panel—time will start counting down.
  3. Select Rinse to wet your dog.
  4. Choose Tearless Shampoo or Oat Shampoo and lather.
  5. Rinse thoroughly, then use Conditioner or Flea & Tick if needed.
  6. Finish with Rinse, then Dryer to blow off excess water.
  7. Use the Vacuum setting if you need to remove water from the floor.
Safety Reminder: Please do not leave pets unattended in the dog wash.
